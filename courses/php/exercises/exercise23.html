Exercise 23: Email Validator

Use regular expressions to validate email addresses.

Objective

Master regular expressions for email validation.

Email Validation Rules

  • Must contain @ symbol
  • Must contain . after @
  • Text before @ (username)
  • Text between @ and . (domain)
  • Text after . (TLD)

Requirements

  • Create regex pattern for email
  • Create validateEmail() function
  • Test with various email formats
  • Provide feedback on validation
